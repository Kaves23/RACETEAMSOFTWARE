QNH — Mean Sea Level Pressure    QFE — Station / Surface Pressure
QNH is adjusted to sea level and used for altimetry. QFE is the raw surface pressure at the measurement altitude. Rising pressure generally indicates improving weather; falling signals deterioration. A drop of >3 hPa in 3 hours = Rapidly Falling (storm risk).
