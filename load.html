      Goodloading overview
      Load optimization engine for space utilization, constraints (GVW, axle loads, CoG), multistop sequencing, vehicle recommendation, and 3D animated loading visualization.

      Key capabilities
      • Optimal space use & sequencing (LIFO for multistop)
      • Constraints: axle loads, GVW, center of gravity
      • Vehicle recommendation from available fleet
      • 3D visualization + PDF plan links

      Use cases
      • Fleet operators & manufacturers: cut freight/fuel, reduce damage, automate plans
      • E‑commerce / 3PL: instant, accurate shipping volume/costs, value‑add optimization
      • TMS/WMS/ERP providers: plug a proven loading engine instead of building in‑house

      Benefits
      • Cost: fewer vehicles, less fuel, lower damage claims, lower labor time
      • Speed: plans in seconds, clear animated instructions for ramp teams
      • Compliance: axle limits, CoG respected
      • Sustainability: fewer trips → lower footprint

      Workflow (concept)
      1) Input: items (dims/weight/stack rules), vehicles (dims/limits)
      2) Compute: optimal plan + constraints checked
      3) Output: placements, utilization %, weight/axle loads, 3D link, PDF
