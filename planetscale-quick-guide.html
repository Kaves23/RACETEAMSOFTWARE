🚀 Get Your PlanetScale Connection Details

1 Skip the Role Creation (For Now)

You're currently on the "Create a role" page. Click the I'll do this later button in the top right corner.

⚠️ Don't worry! PlanetScale will create a default connection for you automatically.

2 Click the "Connect" Button

After clicking "I'll do this later", you'll be on your database dashboard. Look for a button in the top right that says Connect and click it.

3 Find the Dropdown Menu

A connection modal will appear. Look for a dropdown that says "Connect with:"

The dropdown options will include:

  • Prisma
  • Node.js ← Select this one!
  • PHP
  • Python
  • Go
  • And more...

4 Select "Node.js"

After selecting Node.js, you'll see connection code that looks like this:

const connection = mysql.createConnection({ host: 'aws.connect.psdb.cloud', username: 'xxxxxxxxxxxx', password: 'pscale_pw_xxxxxxxxxxxxx', database: 'raceteam-logistics-v5' })

5 Copy Your Credentials

Copy these three values:

  • host: (example: aws.connect.psdb.cloud or us-east.connect.psdb.cloud)
  • username: (a long random string)
  • password: (starts with "pscale_pw_")
⚠️ Important: If you don't see a password, click the "New password" button in the modal to generate one. You can only see it once!

6 Come Back Here

Once you have your credentials, come back to this page and enter them below:
